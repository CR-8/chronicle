# Getting Started with Drone Robotics

Welcome to the fascinating world of drone robotics! This section covers everything from building your first drone to advanced autonomous flight systems, computer vision, and aerial robotics applications.

## üöÅ What is Drone Robotics?

Drone robotics combines aerial vehicles with intelligent control systems. Modern drones can fly autonomously, perform complex tasks, and integrate with ground-based systems for coordinated operations.

### Drone Categories
- **Multirotor**: Quadcopters, hexacopters, octocopters
- **Fixed-wing**: Traditional airplane-style drones
- **VTOL**: Vertical takeoff and landing aircraft
- **Hybrid**: Combined capabilities systems

<Steps>
<Step>
**Choose Your Drone Type**

Start with appropriate complexity:
- **Beginner**: Ready-to-fly (RTF) quadcopter
- **Intermediate**: Build-your-own kit
- **Advanced**: Custom-designed drone
- **Professional**: Industrial/commercial systems
</Step>

<Step>
**Select Components**

Essential drone components:
- **Frame**: Carbon fiber or plastic structure
- **Motors**: Brushless motors with ESCs
- **Propellers**: Correct size and pitch
- **Flight Controller**: Pixhawk, Betaflight, or ArduPilot
- **Power System**: LiPo batteries and regulators
- **Telemetry**: Radio control and monitoring
</Step>

<Step>
**Assembly Process**

Systematic build approach:
1. Mount motors and ESCs to frame
2. Install flight controller
3. Connect power distribution
4. Attach propellers carefully
5. Configure radio system
6. Calibrate sensors and systems
</Step>

<Step>
**Software Setup**

Programming and configuration:
1. Install flight control software
2. Configure PID settings
3. Set up radio calibration
4. Test autonomous modes
5. Implement safety features
</Step>

## üîß Essential Components

### Airframe & Structure
- **Frame**: Carbon fiber, aluminum, or plastic
- **Landing Gear**: Fixed or retractable
- **Protection**: Propeller guards for safety
- **Mounting Points**: For payloads and accessories

### Power System
- **Motors**: Brushless outrunner motors
- **ESCs**: Electronic speed controllers
- **Batteries**: LiPo/Li-ion high discharge rate
- **Power Distribution**: PDB boards
- **Charging System**: Smart chargers and balancers

### Flight Control
- **Flight Controller**: Pixhawk, Naze32, or KK boards
- **IMU/Gyro**: Inertial measurement unit
- **GPS/Compass**: For autonomous navigation
- **Barometer**: Altitude measurement
- **Telemetry**: Real-time data transmission

### Communication
- **Radio Transmitter**: 2.4GHz/915MHz systems
- **Receiver**: Compatible with transmitter
- **Telemetry Module**: MAVLink protocol
- **Ground Station**: Mission planning software

## üõ°Ô∏è Safety & Regulations

<Callout type="warn" title="Drone Safety Guidelines">

**Pre-Flight Checks:**
- [ ] Battery voltage and capacity
- [ ] Propeller condition and balance
- [ ] Control surfaces and linkages
- [ ] GPS lock and satellite count
- [ ] Weather conditions acceptable
- [ ] Emergency procedures reviewed

**Flight Safety:**
- Maintain visual line of sight
- Keep safe distance from people/structures
- Monitor battery levels continuously
- Have emergency landing plan
- Respect no-fly zones and restrictions

**Legal Compliance:**
- Register drone if required
- Follow weight and altitude limits
- Obtain waivers for special operations
- Keep drone in good repair

</Callout>

## üîç Common Challenges

<Callout type="error" title="Drone Building Pitfalls">

**Stability Issues:**
- Incorrect PID tuning
- Imbalanced propellers
- Loose motor mounts
- Vibration problems

**Range Problems:**
- Antenna orientation
- Interference sources
- Low transmitter power
- Poor receiver sensitivity

**Power Issues:**
- Insufficient battery capacity
- High current draw
- Poor power distribution
- Inefficient motors

**GPS Problems:**
- Poor satellite visibility
- Magnetic interference
- Incorrect compass calibration
- Firmware configuration errors

</Callout>
