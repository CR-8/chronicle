## üìö What's Covered

### Foundational Knowledge
- **Motion & Forces**: Physics of movement, torque, power, and energy
- **Introduction**: History, concepts, domains, and classification of robotics

### Electrical Systems
- **Current, Voltage & Resistance**: Electrical fundamentals
- **Power Equations**: Energy calculations and efficiency
- **Circuits**: Series/parallel, load balancing, circuit design

### Power & Components
- **Batteries**: Chemistry, ratings, and protection
- **Motors**: Selection, control, and electronic speed controllers
- **Motor Drivers**: L298N, PWM, and H-bridge configuration

### Control & Perception
- **Microcontrollers**: Arduino, ESP32, Raspberry Pi comparison
- **Motor Control**: Speed, direction, PID algorithms
- **Sensors**: IR, ultrasonic, light, temperature, and feedback systems

### Mechanical Engineering
- **Chassis & Materials**: Aluminum, 3D printing, carbon fiber
- **Transmission**: Gears, chains, belts, and mechanical advantage
- **Fabrication**: Drilling, welding, soldering techniques

### Connectivity & Integration
- **Wiring & Connectors**: AWG gauge, connector types, soldering
- **Crimp Techniques**: Professional connection methods
